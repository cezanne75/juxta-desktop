Juxta now has the ability to upload your comparison set to the web! Your texts and collations will be uploaded to juxtacommons.org where you can view them once you login. You can also choose to share them with others on the web via a link.

If you are new to juxtacommoms.org, click on the "Create Account" below to create a free account.

This service is provided by NINES at the University of Virginia.
